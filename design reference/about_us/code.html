How we started

Trust & Will was founded in 2017 with a simple realization: the traditional legal process for estate planning was broken. It was too expensive, too slow, and too intimidating for most families.

We set out to build a platform that combines legal expertise with modern technology. Today, we're the most trusted name in online estate planning, helping hundreds of thousands of people secure their future without the high costs of a traditional law firm.
